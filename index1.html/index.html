What is an Exoplanet?

An Exoplanet is a planet that orbits a star outside the solar system. They come in various sizes, compositions, and types. Some exoplanets are rocky like Earth, while others are gas giants like Jupiter.

As of now, thousands of exoplanets have been discovered, expanding our understanding of the universe and our search for habitable worlds.
